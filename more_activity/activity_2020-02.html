Japan-Russia Far East Agricultural Business Forum 2020
～Potential of Honey Business Expansion～

Date and Time

February 28, 2020 (Fri) 13:00～16:50

Organizer

National University Corporation CHIBA UNIVERSITY

Venue

Kashiwanoha Conference Center
Hotel & Residence Building 2F, Kashiwa-no-ha Campus
178-4 Wakashiba, Kashiwa, Chiba 277-0871

Participation fee

Free (pre-registration required)


Language

Japanese and Russian (simultaneous interpretation)

Content

This forum aims to share information on challenges and solutions for expanding honey imports from Russian Far East.

Participation from four universities below, and local governments and companies from Russian Far East and Siberian region is anticipated.

  • ・ Primorskaya State Academy of Agriculture（Ussuriysk, Primorsky Krai）
  • ・ Sakhalin State University（Yuzhno-Sakhalinsk, Sakhalin Oblast）
  • ・ Far Eastern State Agrarian University（Blagoveshchensk, Amur Oblast）
  • ・ Novosibirsk State Agrarian University（Novosibirsk, Novosibirsk Oblast）

Program

・Opening speech by Vice President of Chiba University

・Presentations plan to include;

　　- Challenges of expanding honey imports from Russian Far East

　　- Characteristics of beekeeping and honey production in each region and the current export situation

・ Panel discussion

・ Information exchange gathering after the forum (Participation fee: 3,000 yen)
