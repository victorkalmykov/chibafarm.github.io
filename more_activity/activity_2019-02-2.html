Educational exhibition “Study in Japan”

1. Date and Venue

  • February 8, 2019 (Friday) In： Faculty of Education, Pacific National University (Khabarovsk, Russia) Karl Marx St., 68
  • February 9, 2019 (Saturday) In：Pacific National University Campus (Russian Federation · Khabarovsk) Tihookeanskaya St., 136

2. Program

February 8, 2019 (Friday)

Target audience: high school students, university students, parents

Time: 15: 00-17: 00

  • Program
  • １） Opening remarks
  • ２） Participating universities / institutions
  • ３） Individual consultation and Q & A response by participating universities from Japan
  • (We plan to set up booths of participating universities and organizations from Japan)

February 9, 2019 (Saturday)

Target audience: college students, parents

Time: 10: 00-14: 00

  • Program
  • １） Opening remarks
  • ２） Message from the representative of the Pacific National University
  • ３） Outline of study in Japan, introduction of study abroad etc.
  • ４） Study abroad experience report by experienced students in Japan
  • ５） Participating universities / institutions
  • ６） Individual consultation and Q & A response by participating universities from Japan
  • (We plan to set up booths of participating universities and organizations from Japan)
