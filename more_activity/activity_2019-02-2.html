Educational exhibition “Study in Japan”

Information of educational exhibition “Study in Japan”

Introduction

At the beginning of the February 2019, it is planned to conduct an educational exhibition “Study in Japan” on the basis of Pacific State University, Khabarovsk, Russia. The agreement on holding this exhibition was reached at the last meeting of the representatives of PSU and representatives of the Hokkaido University (Sapporo, Japan). 10 Japanese universities are scheduled to attend, including Chiba University.

Exhibition scheduled

  • 8 February 2019 for students on the basis of the Pedagogical Institute of PSU.
  • 9 February 2019 for students on the basis of the intellectual center of PSU.
